Coffee

Product Details

Our coffee is cultivated in the fertile soils of the Himalayan mountains, where the perfect combination of soil, climate, and altitude produces some of the finest coffee beans in the world. Our coffee is made exclusively from Arabica beans, which are handpicked and sun-dried to maintain their natural flavor. We take pride in using traditional processing methods that have been passed down for generations, ensuring that our coffee is of the highest quality.

The taste of our Nepali coffee is distinct and complex, with notes of chocolate, caramel, and a delicate sweetness. It has a smooth and clean finish that is sure to delight your taste buds. Our coffee is perfect for those who appreciate a mild yet flavorful cup of coffee.By purchasing our Nepali coffee, you are not only supporting the livelihoods of small farmers in Nepal, but also contributing to the economic development of the country. We are committed to promoting sustainable and ethical coffee production, while delivering the best quality coffee to our customers.
