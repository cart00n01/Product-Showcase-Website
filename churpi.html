Product Details

Welcome to our website, where you can discover the unique taste of hardened cheese, also known as Churpi. Churpi is a traditional Himalayan cheese that is made by boiling and hardening yak or cow's milk. Our Churpi is made using the traditional method that has been passed down for generations. It is free from any preservatives or artificial ingredients, making it a natural and healthy snack.

Churpi has a unique texture that is both hard and chewy, making it a great snack for those who enjoy a good chew. It has a mild flavor that is slightly salty and nutty, making it a versatile ingredient that can be used in a variety of dishes.
Churpi is an excellent source of protein and calcium, making it a healthy and nutritious snack for all ages. It is also low in fat and calories, making it a guilt-free snack that you can enjoy anytime, anywhere. It is perfect as a snack on its own, or as a topping for salads, soups, and other dishes.
Experience the unique taste and texture of Churpi and discover why it has been a favorite snack in the Himalayan region for centuries. Order now and enjoy the goodness of this traditional Himalayan cheese.
